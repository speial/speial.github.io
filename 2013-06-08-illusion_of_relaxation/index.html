The Illusion of Relaxation

Made from the flyer for Henning Kles' exhibition 'Use your Illusion I&II'.

In 2013 I did a lot of meditation. It made me pretty relaxed. I was speculating as to whether this relaxation was going all the way down to all areas of my subconcious mind -- or was I still a raging tiger deep down inside, falling for an illusion of relaxation?
