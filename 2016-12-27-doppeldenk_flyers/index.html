speial Doppeldenk Flyers

These are some interesting flyers I found recently. For the exhibition 'Totentanz' by the art duo Doppeldenk from Leipzig. The exhibition is currently taking place at the Affenfaust Galerie in Hamburg.

It seems to be the photo of an acrylic painting. Made solely of solid colors and rectangular shapes. They have a glossy coating, which makes the colors even more intense.
